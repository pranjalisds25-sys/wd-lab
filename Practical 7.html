The Life of a Genius

Sir Isaac Newton (1642–1727) was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, and author who is widely recognized as one of the most influential scientists of all time.

Key Contributions

  • Laws of Motion: Established the foundation of classical mechanics.
  • Universal Gravitation: Formulated the law that explains how planets move.
  • Calculus: Independently developed infinitesimal calculus.
  • Optics: Discovered that white light is composed of a spectrum of colors.
